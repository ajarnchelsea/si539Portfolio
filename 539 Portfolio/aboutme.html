Hi! I'm Chelsea.

I am a first year master's student of Human-computer Interaction at the University of Michigan School of Information. I am interested in user experience research and interaction design.

I also represent the School of Information as a Coleman-Munger Fellow at the Munger Graduate Residences. I am a part of our Data Analysis Committee, where I use contextual inquiry to identify opportunities for programmatic growth.

Before starting this program, I taught English in Bangkok, Thailand for three years; studied abroad in Hong Kong for a semester; and traveled through more than 11 countries throughout Asia.
